Open My Heart

A Letter Written in Heartbeats

💋

💋
💋
My dearest Mommy,

"If imagination is the only way to be with you… then I never want to open my eyes. Let me stay in that dream a little longer — the one where your laughter fills every corner of the room and your warmth is the only world I know."

"Every time I sit alone, or even in a crowd, my mind somehow finds its way back to you. Not by choice — it's as if my soul simply refuses to wander anywhere you aren't. You are the compass my heart follows without question."

"My biggest wish is that Allah writes you in my naseeb forever. Not just in this life — in every version of every life I'm given."

"I want to spend centuries loving you… a lifetime getting lost in your beautiful eyes, discovering new constellations in them each time I look. There is a whole universe hiding behind that gaze, and I want to be the only one who has ever truly seen it."

"My beautiful butterfly — you are dangerously beautiful. With the prettiest innocent face that disarms me before I've said a word, and the most mesmerizing soul that I am still learning to understand even after all this time. You ruin me in the gentlest, most exquisite way."

"You are the kind of love poets fail to describe. The kind that lives beyond language, beyond metaphor — the kind that can only be felt."

"When life feels heavy — when the weight of the world settles on my shoulders and silence starts to feel loud — your existence feels like peace wrapped in moonlight. Just the thought of you makes the chaos quieter."

"I could search the whole universe, wander through galaxies yet unnamed, and still never find a soul like yours. You are not a coincidence. You are the most deliberate, most beautiful thing that has ever happened to me."

"Your smile softens my chaos. Your voice calms my storms. In a world that sometimes feels too loud, too sharp, too uncertain — you are the gentlest constant I have ever known."

"Loving you feels like walking into a warm room after a long winter. Like finding something I didn't know I was looking for until it was right in front of me. You make existence feel worth it in ways I struggle to put into words."

"You are home. Not a place — a feeling. A warmth. The kind of home I carry inside my chest every single day."

"And if destiny is kind to me… every road in my life will somehow lead back to you. No matter where the journey takes me, no matter how long the detour — I know in the deepest part of me that every path bends back toward you."

❤

"Thank you for being you. For the softness and the fire. For the patience and the passion. For letting me love you, and for loving me in return in ways I still don't entirely feel deserving of."

"On this day, I want you to know — you are seen. You are cherished. You are adored beyond what words can hold. You are my greatest privilege."

Forever yours, your Hubby ❤️
